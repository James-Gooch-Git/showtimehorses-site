Why Showtime

Everything a show needs, in one app

From the organiser's desk to the ring steward's phone to the rider warming up — everyone sees the same live picture of the day.

Auto-generated riding orders

Fair, random draws in seconds — with trainer grouping, horse recovery gaps, and multi-horse riders handled automatically. No more late-night spreadsheets.

Live arena updates

Every arena, live: who's on track, their time and faults, who's next, and how long until your ride. Riders stop crowding the gate to ask.

One-tap steward scoring

Stewards run a class with a single button. Big targets, high contrast, works on a phone in a field — poles, refusals, scratches and swaps all handled.

Auto day schedules

Generate a full arena-by-arena timetable from your class lists — with clash detection when the same horse is entered across arenas.

Instant results

Leaderboards update the moment a round finishes. Standard, two-phase and jump-off classes all scored correctly, automatically.

Effortless entries

Riders create a profile once, then enter any show in a few taps. Organisers see entries, classes and outstanding paperwork in one dashboard.

How It Works

Show day, simplified

About Us

Built by people who've stood at the ring all day

Showtime started with a frustration familiar to anyone in South African equestrian sport: riding orders drawn up by hand the night before, schedules that drift within the first hour, and riders who spend the whole day asking "how long until my class?"

We're building the tool we wished existed — a single app where organisers set up the show, the system draws fair riding orders automatically, stewards keep each arena moving with one tap, and everyone on the ground sees exactly what's happening in every ring, live.

Showtime supports all disciplines — show jumping, dressage, showing, Western, endurance and more — and works with events run under SASJ, SANESA, Equestrian SA or independently.

Why South Africa first?

International platforms weren't built for how SA shows actually run — school leagues, volunteer stewards, rural showgrounds with patchy signal. Showtime is designed for exactly that: lightweight, offline-tolerant, and simple enough that a first-time volunteer needs zero training.
